Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about joining and participating in GBS.

What is Global Business Social (GBS)?

GBS is a professional networking organization that connects business owners, entrepreneurs, and professionals to help them grow their businesses through referrals, partnerships, and collaborative opportunities. We facilitate meaningful connections that lead to mutual business success.

How do I join a chapter?

Joining is easy! Contact us to find a chapter in your area or explore starting a new one. We'll connect you with local business professionals in your industry and help you get started with networking opportunities right away.

What are the membership benefits?

Members enjoy exclusive access to networking events, referral opportunities, business education workshops, mentorship programs, and our online platform for year-round collaboration. You'll also receive priority support and resources to help grow your business.

How much does membership cost?

Membership fees vary by chapter and location. Contact your local chapter for specific pricing information. We believe in providing exceptional value, and most members see a return on investment within the first few months through increased referrals and business opportunities.

Can I start my own chapter?

Absolutely! We're always looking for passionate business leaders to start new chapters in underserved areas. We provide comprehensive training, ongoing support, and all the resources you need to build a successful networking group in your community.
